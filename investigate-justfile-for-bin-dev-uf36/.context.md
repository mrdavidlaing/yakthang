Investigate replacing bin/dev shell script with a Justfile.

## Background
bin/dev is a ~978-line quality gate script. Just (https://github.com/casey/just) could provide:
- Named recipes instead of subcommands parsed in bash
- Better dependency management between tasks
- Cross-platform compatibility
- Easier to read and maintain

## Questions to Explore
- Would a Justfile cover all bin/dev subcommands (check, lint, test, cx)?
- How does Just handle the complexity analysis integration?
- Can Just recipes compose well enough to replace the current sequential pipeline?
- What would the migration path look like (gradual or big bang)?
- Does the yx project itself use Just anywhere as a reference?
