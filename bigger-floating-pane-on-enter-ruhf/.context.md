When you select a yak in the yak-map plugin and hit enter, it opens a floating Zellij pane showing the yak detail. Currently the floating pane is too small. It should take up nearly all available space — roughly a 2-3 line margin on all sides.

## Acceptance criteria
- The floating pane opened on enter is much larger (nearly full screen)
- 2-3 line margin on top, bottom, left, and right
- Content is easier to read without scrolling
- Existing behavior (what's shown in the pane) is unchanged
